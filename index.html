About

Welcome to La Cuisine de Julie

The authentic taste of our food comes from family recipes and from fresh, simple and tasteful ingredients straight from home. In every plate from La Cuisine de Julie, there is true African culture and flavor.

We proud ourselves in following the traditional recipes used by African families.
